Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 6 apples, sliced
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 pie crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, salt, and lemon juice.
  3. Place the apple mixture into the pie crust.
  4. Cover with top crust and seal the edges.
  5. Bake for 50 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Let it cool before serving.
